About Me

Welcome to my page! My name Jess. For as long as I can remember I've wanted to make video games. But it's always been a private ambition; Something I would pursue in my personal time, never the focus of my career.

In 2021, in the wake of the Coronavirus pandemic, I decided that my career had taken me as far as it could. I believe that happiness comes from achieving goals, and because I no longer had any ambitions for my career, I was no longer happy in my career. I made a decision to commit myself to developing the skills and experience I would to become a professional game developer.

This website is a chronicle of that journey.
